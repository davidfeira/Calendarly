How to Use

  1. Click "Copy JSON" to copy your data
  2. Paste it into an LLM (like Claude) to edit
  3. Example: "Add hockey practice every Tuesday at 6 PM until Dec 18"
  4. Paste the modified JSON back into the editor
  5. Click "Import JSON" to update your calendar
⚠️
Warning: This directly modifies your calendar data. Invalid JSON or incorrect data structure may cause data loss. Make sure to keep a backup copy before importing!
